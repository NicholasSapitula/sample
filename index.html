CHARACTERS OF NOLI ME TANGERE
Crisostomo Ibarra Studied in Europe not to become higher in social position, but to help his country, the Philippines.
Don Rafael Ibarra Father of Crisostomo Ibarra. Very rich and had many admirers and enemies. Died in prison due to multiple false accusations.
Kapitan Tiago Powerful person in government. Likes to hold parties and events. Hates merchants and traders.
Padre Damaso Very greedy priest of the church. Often seen fighting to maintain his power via encouraging the Indios to not pursue education or even learn to read.
